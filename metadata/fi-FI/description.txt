ChatSecure is an open-source encrypted messaging application that uses Cypherpunks' Off-the-Record protocol to secure a communication channel over XMPP (Google Talk, Jabber, etc) or Oscar (AIM).
Fork me on Github! https://github.com/chrisballinger/ChatSecure-iOS
Multiprotocol Chat
ChatSecure works with both XMPP and AIM accounts.
Strong Encryption
The Off-the-Record library provides a strong base for encrypted communication.
Compatible
You can securely chat with other programs such as Adium or Pidgin.
